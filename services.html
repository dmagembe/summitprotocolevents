Executive Ushering

Our flagship service — impeccably trained ushers embodying the Summit Protocol standard of grace, warmth, and precision.

  • Full pre-event briefing & rehearsal
  • Tailored uniform in your event colour scheme
  • Guest registration & seating management
  • Programme guide distribution
  • Accessibility & VIP routing support

Protocol Management

Comprehensive event protocol coordination for state functions, diplomatic dinners, and high-profile corporate occasions.

  • Full protocol run-of-show design
  • Diplomatic & state precedence management
  • MC briefing & stage direction support
  • Ceremonial order management
  • Liaison with security & official delegations

VIP Guest Management

Discreet and highly personalised management for heads of state, executives, royalty, and distinguished guests.

  • Dedicated personal usher per VIP
  • Arrival, reception & departure management
  • Coordination with personal security details
  • Private lounge & holding room management
  • Real-time communication throughout event

Corporate Functions

From AGMs and product launches to investor summits and award ceremonies — staffing that reflects your organisation's standing.

  • Corporate branding alignment in all attire

  • Award ceremony & stage management
  • Post-event guest relations & debrief

Weddings & Social Events

Bringing sophisticated order and elegance to your most treasured personal milestones.

  • Guest seating & programme management
