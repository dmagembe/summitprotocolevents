Our Foundation

Built on Precision,
Driven by Purpose

"Protocol is not procedure. It is the visible form of invisible values."

Summit protocol is not just a name that identifies us, a name that symbolizes what we have overcome to get here. From volunteering at national and internal events, to training in anticipation of nothing, to bringing together teams of like minded people like our COO, and now executing events with precision, quality and love. We keep waiting for the opportunity to serve another gathering because we are called to put a smile through service, a smile to be remembered, and event to stay at the heart of guests.

Summit Protocol Uganda is a premier, highly distinguished protocol and executive ushering team specialising in VIP handling, event coordination, and ceremonial management for high-level government, diplomatic, corporate, and institutional engagements.

We set out to change how event service is experienced in East Africa. From our earliest engagements managing corporate dinners for leading firms, we cultivated a philosophy centred on presence, poise, and genuine hospitality.

Today, Summit Protocol stands as one of Uganda's most trusted names in executive event ushering — having served government ministries, multinational corporations, embassies, and private clients who share one expectation: nothing but the best.
